Faith

A Generation That Walks By Faith

Kevonie Bishop is deeply committed to seeing a generation walk by faith and develop a deep, personal relationship with Jesus Christ. Through his ministry, he inspires young adults to embrace a life of faith, encouraging them to trust in God's plan for their lives. Kevonie's passion is to see individuals grow in their faith, equipping them with the tools and knowledge needed to navigate life's challenges with unwavering trust in Jesus. He believes that by empowering young people to develop their faith, they can discover their purpose and make a lasting impact in the world for the glory of God.
