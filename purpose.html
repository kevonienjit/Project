Purpose

Kevonie Bishop is wholeheartedly committed to helping young adults discover their purpose in God. Through his ministry, he guides individuals on a journey of self-discovery, encouraging them to seek God's will for their lives. Kevonie believes that everyone has a unique purpose and gifts from God, and he is dedicated to helping young adults uncover and develop these gifts. By empowering them to align their lives with God's purpose, Kevonie inspires young adults to live fulfilling lives that bring glory to God.
